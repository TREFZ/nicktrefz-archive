TopCoder Software Tour

TopCoder's approach to software development was revolutionary, which also meant it was difficult for newcomers to grasp. Our research showed that many people came to our site, found an abundance of information, but often left with thoughts like "I have no idea what TopCoder does" or "I didn't know that TopCoder makes software". After researching successful in-person introductions with our sales team, I suggested we provide a "tour", a low level introduction to our process, first presenting the most basic message ("We build software.") and then gradually providing more detail on what made us different.

I molded the idea into a series of pages, then designed and coded them into a working HTML tour.

History/Distribution Graphs (Flash)

TopCoder competitors earned a rating that changed each time they competed. To complement our library of statistics, I suggested an interactive graph that mapped each competitor's rating history over the course of their career. I gave the developers a sample XML page to use as a template for live data feeds, and let them know that if they produced the feed, I would provide the Flash to parse and visually display it. In addition, each node on the graph would react to mouseovers by displaying information about that competition, and clicking the node would bring the user to the overall results of that competition. The graph now rests prominently on every member's profile page.

I followed up that project with a distribution graph that would sort the entire community into rating "buckets" and then mark the location of a given member, to help visualize how that competitor stacked up against the rest of his peers.
